Ingredients

  • 8 ounce cream cheese
  • 14 ounce sweetened condensed milk
  • 5 ounce instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 3 cups cold skim milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 8 ounce frozen whipped topping
  • 12 ounce vanilla wafers
  • 4 bananas

Steps

  • Beat the cream cheese till fluffy
  • Add condensed milk and pudding mix
  • Mix in cold milk and vanilla
  • Fold in half of the whipped topping
  • Add the vanilla wafers to a tray
  • Put a slice of banana on each wafer
  • Cover with the pudding mixture
  • Add the remaining whipped topping
  • Cool in the refrigerator for 3 hours before serving
