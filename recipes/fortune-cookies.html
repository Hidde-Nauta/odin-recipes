Ingredients

  • 1 large egg white
  • 0.125 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 0.25 cup all-purpose flour
  • 0.25 cup white sugar
  • 1 pinch of salt

Steps

  • Preheat the over to 200C
  • Mix the egg white and vanilla until foamy
  • Sift in flour, sugar and salt
  • Spread batter in two 3-inch-diameter circles
  • Bake for about 5 minutes
  • Fold into fortune cookies
  • Repeat for the rest of the batter
